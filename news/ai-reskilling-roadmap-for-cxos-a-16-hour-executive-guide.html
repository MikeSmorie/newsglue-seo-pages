In an era where artificial intelligence (AI) is reshaping the fabric of industries, the introduction of a 16-hour AI reskilling roadmap for CXOs marks a pivotal moment. Redefining Leadership in the AI Era

The move towards prioritizing AI education at the executive level represents a significant shift in how authority and trust are allocated within organizations. Traditionally, technological fluency might have been delegated to IT departments or digital innovation teams. However, this paradigm is changing. The ability to make informed decisions about AI investments and implementations is rapidly becoming a cornerstone of effective leadership. This shift suggests that the leaders of tomorrow are those who today embrace the opportunity to reskill, ensuring their decisions are informed by a deep understanding of AI's potential and limitations.
